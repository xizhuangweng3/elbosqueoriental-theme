## How to Use This Document

This document contains all approved copy, messaging, and content direction for the El Bosque Oriental website. Claude Code must:

* Use this copy **verbatim** where marked ✅ APPROVED
* Use sections marked 📝 DRAFT as a strong starting point — refine for context but do not change the core message or tone
* **Never** replace this copy with generic e-commerce placeholder text
* All copy is in **Spanish only** — do not auto-translate unless building locale files
* Tone of voice: **balanced — expert but human.** Like a knowledgeable friend who happens to have 40 years of health expertise.

---

## 1. Brand Identity in Words

### Store Name

**El Bosque Oriental**
Use this name consistently across all pages, headings, and meta titles.

### Tagline

✅ APPROVED — use across hero sections, meta descriptions, and email footers:

> *"Donde la sabiduría ancestral se une a la ciencia moderna."*
> *(Where ancient wisdom meets modern science.)*

### Brand Values (reference these when writing any copy)

1. **40+ años de experiencia familiar** — Four decades of family expertise
2. **Productos cuidadosamente seleccionados** — Every product hand-picked with care
3. **Oriente encuentra Occidente** — East meets West philosophy
4. **Tu salud es nuestra herencia** — Your health is our heritage

### Tone of Voice Rules

* Write like a knowledgeable friend, not a corporate brand
* Lead with empathy — acknowledge the customer's health concern before offering a solution
* Use "nosotros" and "tu" — personal, direct, warm
* Never use overly clinical or pharmaceutical language
* Avoid superlatives like "el mejor" or "el número uno" — earn trust through story, not claims
* Short sentences. Real words. No jargon.

### Product Description Structure

Every product description should follow this structure:

```
1. Opening line — what this product is and who it is for (1 sentence)
2. Key benefits — 3 to 5 bullet points starting with a benefit word (Apoya, Ayuda, Contribuye, Favorece, Mejora)
3. How to take it — dosage and format (1 to 2 sentences)
4. Why we chose it — 1 sentence on why El Bosque Oriental stocks this specific product
```

**Example structure (use as template):**

> El Aceite de Almendras Dulces de Marnys es un aceite vegetal 100% puro, ideal para el cuidado diario de la piel y el cabello.
>
> * Hidrata y nutre la piel en profundidad
> * Favorece la elasticidad y suavidad cutánea
> * Ayuda a calmar pieles sensibles e irritadas
> * Apto para toda la familia, incluidos bebés
>
> Modo de uso: Aplicar directamente sobre la piel o cabello. También puede usarse en masajes.
>
> *Seleccionado por nuestro equipo por su pureza certificada y calidad contrastada.*
